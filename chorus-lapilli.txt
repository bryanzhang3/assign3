Added isAdjacent(a, b) which turns linear indices to (row, col) and returns true when two squares are adjacent when their row and column indices each differ by at most 1 (and they aren't the same square).
Added a third piece of state, `selected`, holding the index of a picked-up piece or null. handleClick branches on phase:
- Placement (< 3 own pieces): same as tic-tac-toe placement.
- Movement, nothing selected: click must be one of your own pieces to become the selection; anything else ignored.
- Movement, something selected: destination must be empty AND adjacent to `selected`; otherwise reset selection to null to meet spec requirements.
